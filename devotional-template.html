The Currency of Integrity

"Better is the poor that walketh in his uprightness, than he that is perverse in his ways, though he be rich."
— Proverbs 28:6 (KJV)

Marketplace Insight

In the marketplace, your integrity is your most valuable asset. While many believe currency is the foundation of business, it is actually trust. Trust is the lubricant of commerce.

Handling office politics God's way means choosing truth over convenience. It means being the same person in the boardroom as you are in the prayer room. When you build with integrity, you build a foundation that can withstand any economic storm.

Marketplace Principle

Integrity is not just a moral choice; it is a competitive advantage that builds sustainable wealth.

Action Step

Integrity Audit: Recall a recent situation where you were tempted to compromise the truth for a quick win. Revisit that decision and ensure your actions aligned with Kingdom values. If not, make it right.

Prayer

"Father, help me to walk in uprightness in all my dealings. Let my 'yes' be 'yes' and my 'no' be 'no'. Grant me the courage to choose integrity even when it costs me, knowing that my reward comes from You. Amen."
